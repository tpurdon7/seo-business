# AI SEO Skill Placeholder

Use this file for Better Search GEO and AEO rules.

GEO means generative engine optimisation: making a business easy for AI search systems to understand, summarize, and recommend.

AEO means answer engine optimisation: making a page ready to answer direct questions with clear, extractable content.

Initial checks:

- Clear entity positioning.
- Service or category clarity.
- Location and market relevance.
- Proof and authority signals.
- Comparison, category, and buyer-fit language.
- FAQ or question-answer structure.
- Concise definitions.
- Descriptive headings.
- Schema and answer readiness.

Future expansion:

- AI citation tracking.
- Third-party source mapping.
- LLMs.txt recommendations.
- Competitor comparison for AI answers.
